Halal, Quality & Food Safety Policy

Gumcorp (Pvt.) Ltd is committed to produce and provide Quality, Hygienic and Halal Product (Gum Base) that meet and exceed the needs and expectation of our valued customers

In this regard, we ensure:

  • Continual improving our product quality, operational effectiveness and technology.
  • Product is always prepared under hygienic conditions that do not expose it to the risk of contamination.
  • Focus on customer satisfaction.
  • Comply with the national & international standards, legal and regulatory applicable requirements.
  • Comply with applicable Tayyab & Taharah practices, Good Hygiene Practices & Good Manufacturing practices.
  • Continuously improving the Management Systems and adopting different management tools, techniques, standards, rules & regulation
  • Provide equal opportunities & pleasant working environment for all employees.
  • Provide appropriate and sufficient resources, including training, in order to achieve targeted performance levels on an ongoing basis.

HSE Policy

The management & staff of Gumcorp (Pvt.) Ltd. is committed to conduct all operations with the utmost regard for the Health and Safety of its employees, its customers and the public, without creating unacceptable health, safety and environmental risks.

We will continually strive to:

  • Provide safe and healthy working conditions for the prevention of work-related injury and ill health.
  • Eliminate hazards and reduce OH&S risks.
  • Communicate health, safety and environmental policies of the company to all employees, clients, with whom the company may be associated and the general public.
  • Eliminate and effectively control activities to protect environment including pollution prevention which can have adverse impact on the environment.
  • Conduct programs to safeguard employees, customers and the public from injury and health hazards.
  • Comply with all applicable obligation including local, provincial and federal laws, regulations and other requirements.
  • Provide training to all employees to enable them to protect their health, to work safely and to protect the environment.
  • Continually improve our HSE performance by maintaining a management system and implementing it without compromise or prejudice.
  • Consultation and participation of workers or workers representatives at every level.
